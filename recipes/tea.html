HOW TO MAKE A CUP OF TEA




Description

A cup of tea is a comforting beverage made by steeping tea
leaves or a tea bag in hot water. It offers a soothing aroma,
a rich flavor, and can be enjoyed plain or with milk, sugar,
or honey. Perfect for relaxation, energy, or warmth during any time of day.



Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea Leaves or Tea Bag
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk Powder


STEPS

1. **Boil Water*
Heat water in a kettle or pan until it comes to a boil.
2. **Prepare the Cup**
Place a tea bag or loose tea leaves in a teacup.
3. **Pour the Water**
Carefully pour the hot water over the tea bag or tea leaves.
4. **Steep the Tea**
Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on your preferred strength.
5. **Remove the Tea Bag**
Take out the tea bag or strain the loose tea leaves.
6. **Add Milk and Sugar (Optional)**
Add milk, sugar, honey, or lemon according to your taste preference.
7. **Stir and Serve**
Stir well and enjoy your warm, comforting cup of tea!
